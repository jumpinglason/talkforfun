Talk4Fun

Talk to a stranger — by text or by voice call. No sign-up, no camera, ever.

How it works

  • Pick a name — nobody needs your real one.
  • Add what you're into, or leave it blank for a random match.
  • Chat by text, or start a voice call if you both want to.
  • Leave anytime — nothing is saved once you go.
